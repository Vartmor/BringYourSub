## How It Works

1. **All processing happens locally** in your browser
2. **Direct API calls** are made only to:
   - `youtube.com` - to fetch video transcripts
   - `api.openai.com` - to translate subtitles (using YOUR key)
3. **No intermediary servers** - we don't operate any backend
